The fluffiest tomato and egg recipe

The fluffiest tomato and egg recipe that will make your mum proud. This recipe is comfort food in a hug

Ingredients:

  • Tomato
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps:

  • Chop the tomatoes
  • Crack 3 eggs and beat them with a whisk
  • Mix the tomatoes into the bowl with the eggs
  • Heat oil up in a wok
  • Add the tomato-egg mixture into the wok
  • Add tomato sauce to finish
