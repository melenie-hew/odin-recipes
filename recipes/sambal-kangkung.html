The best sambal kangkung recipe

This dish's aroma will fill your house with joy

Ingredients:

  • 1 g kangkong (water spinach)
  • 1 tbsp belachan
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp sugar
  • 2-3 tbsps vegetable oil
  • sambal paste

Steps:

  • Chop the kangkung
  • Heat the belacan up in a wok
  • Add salt and sugar
  • Heat oil up in a wok
  • Add in the oil
  • Add the kangkung/li>
  • Mix in sambal
